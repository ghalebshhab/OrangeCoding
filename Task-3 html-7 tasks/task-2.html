Petra

Petra is a historical and archaeological city in southern Jordan. It is famous for its rock-cut architecture and water conduit system. Petra is also known as the "Rose City" due to the color of the stone from which it is carved, Petra was established as early as 312 BC as the capital city of the Nabataean Kingdom. It is a symbol of Jordan, as well as Jordan's most-visited tourist attraction.
